\begin{cnabstract}{多智能体强化学习；独立近端策略优化；博弈对抗；奖励塑形；循环神经网络}

这里是中文摘要内容。摘要应概括论文的主要研究内容、方法、结果和结论，
字数在400字左右。摘要应简明扼要，突出论文的创新点和主要贡献。

多智能体强化学习（Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning, MARL）是人工智能领域的重要研究方向，
在无人机博弈对抗等复杂决策场景中具有广泛的应用前景。
本文基于 Godot 引擎构建了二维多智能体博弈环境《Tiny Swords》，
采用独立近端策略优化（Independent Proximal Policy Optimization, IPPO）算法训练智能体，
研究了不同奖励函数设计和观测空间配置对智能体策略学习的影响。
实验结果表明，经过奖励塑形和循环神经网络增强的智能体能够涌现出复杂的博弈与协作行为。

\end{cnabstract}

\begin{enabstract}{Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning; Independent PPO; Game-Theoretic Adversarial; Reward Shaping; Recurrent Neural Network}

This is the English abstract. It should correspond to the Chinese abstract,
with approximately the same content and length.

Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning (MARL) is an important research direction in artificial intelligence,
with broad application prospects in complex decision-making scenarios such as unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) adversarial games.
This paper constructs a 2D multi-agent game environment called "Tiny Swords" based on the Godot engine,
and employs the Independent Proximal Policy Optimization (IPPO) algorithm to train agents.
The influence of different reward function designs and observation space configurations on agent strategy learning is studied.
Experimental results show that agents enhanced with reward shaping and recurrent neural networks
are able to emerge complex game-theoretic and cooperative behaviors.

\end{enabstract}
